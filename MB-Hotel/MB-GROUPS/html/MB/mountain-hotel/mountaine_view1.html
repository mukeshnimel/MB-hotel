Our Location

Mountain View Hotel is located at the peak of Ankleshwar Hills in Bharuch, Gujarat, offering unparalleled views of the Narmada River valley. Our secluded location provides privacy and tranquility while being just 30 minutes from Bharuch city center.

Getting Here

By Air: Nearest airport is Vadodara Airport (BDQ) - 90km away

By Train: Bharuch Junction (BH) - 15km from hotel

By Road: Well-connected by NH48 with ample parking available

Our concierge can arrange private transfers from any arrival point.
