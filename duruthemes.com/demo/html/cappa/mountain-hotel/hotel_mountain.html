Experience Luxury in the Mountains

Welcome to Mountain View Hotel, where breathtaking landscapes meet unparalleled hospitality. Our hotel is strategically located to offer you the most spectacular views of the surrounding mountain ranges, with each suite meticulously designed to bring the outdoors in through floor-to-ceiling windows.

Since opening in 2015, Mountain View Hotel has become the premier destination for those seeking tranquility, adventure, and luxury in equal measure. Our award-winning architecture blends seamlessly with the natural environment, creating a harmonious retreat from the everyday.

Whether you're here for our world-class spa, gourmet dining, or simply to soak in the majestic views, every moment at Mountain View Hotel is designed to be unforgettable.

Our Location

Mountain View Hotel is located at the peak of Ankleshwar Hills in Bharuch, Gujarat, offering unparalleled views of the Narmada River valley. Our secluded location provides privacy and tranquility while being just 30 minutes from Bharuch city center.

Getting Here

By Air: Nearest airport is Vadodara Airport (BDQ) - 90km away

By Train: Bharuch Junction (BH) - 15km from hotel

By Road: Well-connected by NH48 with ample parking available

Our concierge can arrange private transfers from any arrival point.
